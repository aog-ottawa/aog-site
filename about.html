About Us

Discover our mission, vision, and the heart of our community

Our Mission

To make disciples of Jesus Christ who live out their faith through worship, fellowship, service, and the sharing of the Gospel with our community and the world.

Our Vision

To be a vibrant, Spirit-filled community that transforms lives through the power of the Gospel, equips believers for ministry, and makes a lasting impact in Ottawa and beyond.

What We Believe

The Bible

We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible Word of God and our ultimate authority for faith and practice.

The Trinity

We believe in one God eternally existing in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

Salvation

We believe salvation is by grace through faith in Jesus Christ, who died for our sins and rose again.

Holy Spirit

We believe in the present ministry of the Holy Spirit, who empowers believers for spiritual life and service.

Our Story

Assemblies of God Ottawa has been serving the Ottawa community for over 60 years. Founded in the heart of the city, our church has grown into a diverse family of believers united by our love for God and our desire to serve others. Through the decades, we have remained committed to our foundational principles while adapting to meet the changing needs of our community.

Today, we continue to be a place where people from all walks of life can find hope, healing, and purpose through Jesus Christ. Our doors are always open to newcomers, and we invite you to join us on this incredible journey of faith.

Our Values

Worship

We believe worship is not just what we do, but who we are - living lives that honor God in everything we think, say, and do.

Community

We are committed to building authentic relationships and caring for one another as we grow together in our spiritual journey.

Service

We believe every believer is called to serve, and we actively seek opportunities to serve both our church family and the broader community.

Growth

We are committed to personal and spiritual growth, encouraging each person to discover and develop their God-given gifts and calling.
